The World of OUTLAW RP

The Year is 1899

The age of the gunslinger is drawing to a close, but the dying embers of the wild west burn the hottest. The federal government, backed by wealthy eastern magnates and the ruthless Pinkerton Detective Agency, is systematically crushing the remaining outlaw gangs.

In the territories of New Hanover, Ambarino, Lemoyne, and New Austin, civilization is encroaching. Telegraph lines cut across the skies, and the smoke of locomotives chokes the pristine valleys. Yet, in the shadows of the saloons and the hidden canyons, outlaws, rustlers, and revolutionaries refuse to bow to the coming century.

State of the Territories

New Hanover

The heartland. Valentine struggles under the weight of livestock barons and a corrupt local constabulary. It is a hotspot for rustlers and bar brawls.

Lemoyne

Deep south corruption. The bustling city of Saint Denis offers high society and high-stakes crime, contrasting sharply with the treacherous, moonshine-soaked swamps of Bayou Nwa.

New Austin

The unforgiving desert. A haven for those running from the law. Armadillo is suffering from outbreaks of disease, and Tumbleweed operates under a dictator-like Sheriff.

Server Rules & Territorial Laws

OOC Guidelines and In-Character Penal Code. Ignorance is no excuse.

General Roleplay (OOC)

1. Value of Life (NVL)

You must value your character's life at all times. If you are outnumbered or have a weapon drawn on you while unarmed, you must comply. Do not act like a fearless action hero.

2. Metagaming & Stream Sniping

Using out-of-character (OOC) information in-character (IC) is strictly banned. Watching a streamer playing on the server to find their location or plans will result in an immediate permanent ban.

3. Breaking Character

You must stay in character at all times. If there is a rule break or a bug, play through the scenario and report it on Discord later using a ticket. Never say "Press muscle muscle E" or "My game crashed" in voice chat.

4. Crime & Combat Rules

RDM: Killing another player without proper roleplay initiation or a valid, build-up RP reason is prohibited. You must have a verbal exchange before shooting starts.
Limits: Max 6 outlaws in active major scenes (Banks, Trains). Min 4 LEOs online required for bank robbery.
Safe Zones: Medical clinics and spawn locations. Committing violent crimes or taking hostages here is forbidden.

VI. Wildlife & Land Violations (Game Warden Law)

Hunting Hours

Legal Hours: 5:00 AM – 8:00 PM
Allowed After Hours: Coyotes, Wolves, Raccoons.

Required License

Must Have: A valid Hunting License (covers Standard hunting, Fishing, Legendary animal hunting).

Firearms & Ammunition Rules

Big Game: Rifles, Repeaters, Bows, High-Velocity Ammo.
Small Game: Varmint Rifle / .22, Bows, Shotguns with shells.
Waterfowl: Bows.

General Hunting Rules

  • No hunting from public roads mounted or unmounted, wagons, or boats.
  • No hunting within 50 yards (45 meters) of homes/businesses without written permission.
  • No leaving pelts behind (wildlife waste).
  • All wounded animals must be mercy-killed.
  • Endangered (Do Not Kill): American Bison (non-legendary), American Beaver, Bald Eagle.

Hunting Fines (Tier System)

1st Offense: Warning or $50 ($100+ for severe like explosives)
2nd Offense: $200-$250 + Suspension + Gear Confiscation + 3-6 Months Jail
3rd Offense: $500 + License Revoked + Gear Confiscation + 6-12 Months Jail
Killing Endangered Species: $1500 fine + license revoked + firearm confiscation + 12 months jail.

Fishing Laws

Hours: Anytime (Night fishing requires lantern).
Endangered Aquatic: Starfish, Octopus, Squid, Stingray, Hammerhead Shark, Baby Whale.
Rules: No fishing near restricted homes/businesses, no fishing from railroad bridges, no explosives/firearms.

Court & Execution Notes
